AI Ping Pong Game









Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself about 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Press the play button and again move yourself 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop
  5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as you move your right wrist
